Is Inflation always Bad?

Inflation is often intepreted as a plague - but is it truly the barrier to our prosperity, or merely a misunderstood saviour? Well, this question truly is a torture. However, there exists no absolute correct answer to this question. Instead debates has been ongoing due to inflation's different effects based on its rate, causes, and whether its anticipated or not

Lets start off with the very basics; what is inflation? Well in easy words, its how much more expensive goods become upon certain change in the country's economy. In more technical terms, inflation is the sustained increase in the general price level within an economy over time.

Commonly perceived negatively due to rising prices, it is important that we understand that inflation holds great responcibility in encouraging investments and incentivising costumer spending in the contemporary time.
